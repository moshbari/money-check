The one big idea from the video

Get the money math right first

Most people obsess over how to get customers — the ads, the posts, the DMs. Hormozi says do the opposite: first make sure that when a customer shows up, you make good money.

Get a customer worth far more than it costs to get them, and earn that money back fast. Do that, and almost any way of finding customers works. Skip it, and no clever marketing can save you — you just lose money quicker.

6 easy ways to make more

Already running? Pull one of these

You don't need a new business. More profit almost always comes from one of these six simple moves.

Pick a lane

Cheap for everyone, or pricey for a few

Hormozi's rule: pick one end and commit. The stuck-in-the-middle businesses are the ones that die.

💎

Pricey, for a few

Sell something premium to a small group who really want it. Big profit per sale funds everything — and you learn faster from serious buyers.

🛒

Cheap, for everyone

Sell something inexpensive to lots of people and win on volume. Great — once you know the money math already works.

⚠️

Avoid the messy middle

Too expensive to be a bargain, too cheap to be premium. No standout price, no room to grow — this is where businesses quietly bleed out.
